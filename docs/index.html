Our Dance Revolution

As a professional dancer for 8 years, I struggled with disconnected systems for class scheduling, performance archives, and student communication. Transitioning to tech through ALX's Software Engineering program has given me the opportunity to bridge this gap.

This project combines my passion for dance and technology in creating a unified platform I wish existed during my performing years. While it's part of my ALX portfolio project, it's become much more than just a tool for easy accessibility.
